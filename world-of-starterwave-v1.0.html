Welcome to World of StarterWave!

v1.0 Documented on 01/03/2026 by Harsha.

  • Our Purpose:

    Build a Large Team of Web Developers for at least 1 Year.

    Every Team member must be capable of independently running a 3-5 members Sub Team.

    We need to Scale Teams & Team Members Slowly. So that the World lives Organized.

  • Very Very Strict Rules:

    Rule #1: In every Project, Team members should know and Explain Clearly about their Task. And the task must have clear Definition of Done.

    Rule #2: If a Person Delayed their Tasks for 3 Consecutive Projects then they must be Removed from the World of StarterWave.

    Rule #3: Every Month, if all Team Members in a Team are Perfectly Aligned with the World then Only they can Add 1 new Team Member in the Team.

  • Celebrations:

    Every Month on One Sunday, we all meet and that day is No Work & No Mobile Day.

    We enjoy Playing Games, Watching Movies, Casual Discussions or Go to some where.

    No Work & Mobile Day exists only upto 4 Hours, no more Long.
