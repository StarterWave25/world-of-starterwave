Interactive Interview

Documented on 22/03/2026 by Harsha.

  • Why you need to Participate in Interactive Interview?

    It will help you to improve the English Speaking Skills.

    And it also improves the Clarity in Explaination.

  • When these Interactive Interview should be held?

    After Completion of a Weekly Project and Posting about it in LinkedIn by a Team, all Team Members should gather in ZOOM Meeting (Online) or Offline.

    It should be Conducted only for 1 Hour.

  • What you must do in the Interactive Interview?

    1. Explain What you did in the Project Clearly.

    2. Explain What you have Learnt new things Clearly.

    3. After Completion of above 2 things by all Team Members, you must ask at Least One Question to anyone.

  • Rules you must Follow:

    1. If you Enter into the Interactive Interview then Every Word you Speak must be in English.

    2. Team Members can also ask Questions to Team Leader, No Hierachies All are Equal.

    3. No Other Discussions should be Discussed in the Interactive Interview, 1 Hour Completely Focused only on Interactive Interview.
